Ski Runs

The Lowers consist of beginner trails, as well as many intermediate trails mixed with some advanced runs. There are four lifts out of the base area: Lift 1, which services a beginner learning area; Lift 2, which services more advanced beginner terrain, along with intermediate runs; Lift 3, which services all intermediate to advanced trails; Left 4, which services the Playground, our version of play in the snow. The Playground is designed to challenge riders and allow them to be creative. All chairs require each rider to have their own lift ticket.
