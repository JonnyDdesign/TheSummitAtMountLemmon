The Lodge

The Summit at Mount Lemmon ski resort is an amazing ski destination located in the Santa Catalina Mountains near Tucson, Arizona. This family-friendly lodge is an ideal place to take a break from the summer heat and enjoy a relaxing winter getaway. The Summit at Mount Lemmon ski resort boasts over 400 acres of terrain, with runs suitable for all skill levels. The resort also offers a variety of amenities, including a full-service lodge, ski shop, and ski rentals. The Summit at Mount Lemmon ski resort is a great place to bring the family for a fun day of skiing and snowboarding. The resort also offers night skiing, so you can take advantage of the cooler temperatures and longer days to practice your turns. The Summit at Mount Lemmon ski resort is a great place to spend a winter vacation, and with all the amenities, you're sure to have a fantastic time!
